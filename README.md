# 🚀 FocusFlow

**Focus. Plan. Get things done.**

FocusFlow is a simple and beautiful app that helps you stay productive by combining a **Pomodoro timer** with a **daily task checklist** — all in one place.

---

## ✨ What you can do

* ✅ Organize your daily tasks
* ⏱️ Stay focused with Pomodoro sessions
* 🌍 Use the app in your language (EN / PT / ES)
* 🌙 Enjoy a clean, minimal design

---

## 🎯 Why FocusFlow?

Most productivity apps are either too complex or too limited.

FocusFlow gives you exactly what you need:

> **Clarity + Focus — without distractions**
